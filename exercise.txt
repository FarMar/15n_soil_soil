Practical exercise (due 3pm 10-08-2018):
Work through your selected data set, or one of the data sets in Visualisation.

1) Set up a git repository for your analysis. Be sure to commit regularly and push daily.

2) Tracking your work with git, make your data tidy.

3) Explore the data through visualisation (min 5 plots), and develop a final image that tells the data's story.

4) Make a KnitR report with your code and plots.

5) Present to your classmates.
